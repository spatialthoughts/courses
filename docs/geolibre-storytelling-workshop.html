Introduction

GeoLibre is a free and open-source tool for cloud-native geospatial workflows that run in your browser. It is a full featured GIS that can be used for visualizing, exploring, and analyzing geospatial data. One of the stand-out features of the platform is the ability to author interactive story maps and render 3D tours. This workshop gives you hands-on experience with GeoLibre to create and publish story maps using geospatial data.
